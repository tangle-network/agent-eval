# Resolutions

All twelve findings recorded by the two prior audits were fixed and covered by focused tests.

- Candidate history and provenance retain the exact typed edit that produced each measurement.
- Model-authored confidence and gain forecasts do not suppress evidence-linked candidates by default.
- Baselines and candidates must cover the exact designed cell count; partial or non-finite results cannot win.
- Every generation mutates the best complete surface measured across the run.
- Bounded history retains promoted, high-scoring, and low-scoring candidates.
- The provider response schema enforces the effective candidate count.
- GEPA reflects on the measured incumbent that is actually being edited.
- Mixed finite and non-finite judge results are rejected.
- Provenance records and validates parent identity, measured delta, eligibility, coverage, surface bytes, and the final winner chain.
- Regressions are never labeled promoted.
- Known scenario identifiers are pseudonymized across all author-visible fields, including dimension keys.
- Scenario pseudonyms must be one-to-one and collision-free.

Independent API review also confirmed that findings cite real surface-generation pairs, the incumbent's actual measurement generation is retained, objectives match raw-score maximization, measured scores stay within their declared range, and impossible forecasts cannot enter residual history.
